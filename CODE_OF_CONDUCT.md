# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and constructive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its mission

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or work
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action to address behavior that is harmful to the community or the project.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and any other project-related communication.
